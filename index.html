About Internship Program

Our internship program began on May 5, 2023 when we attended DLaunch(Global Internship Program Orientation). Some of us travelled to new cities and were able to explore and meet some amazing fellow interns and Deloitte professionals. We are all working in different service areas within Deloitte Global Technology, but without a doubt we are learning and growing a lot, both personaly and professionally. Out first week was packed with events and orientation programs, such as learning more about different service areas and impact Day. Impact day allowed us to help out our community through activities like gardening, painting, recording audiobooks, and more . We were also given the opportunity to travel to Dallas, Texas where we participated in an innovation challenge Workshop and visits Deloitte University for intern conference. This amazing experience allowed us to make a lot of important connections and undergo professional training. With our internship ending on August 11, we are sad to see it go but glad that we've had the experience and excited to see what the next few weeks hold!
